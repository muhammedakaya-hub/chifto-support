# Chifto: Binary Puzzle — Support

Need help with Chifto? You're in the right place.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do puzzles work?**
Fill every cell with one of two symbols. No more than two of the same symbol can sit side by side, and every row/column must contain an equal number of each symbol. Special "=" and "×" clues tell you when two cells must match or differ.

**I ran out of hints. What do I do?**
Watch a short rewarded ad to earn an extra hint, or purchase a puzzle pack from the Shop for more puzzles and hints.

**How do I remove ads?**
Go to Shop and purchase any "Flex" pack, or the standalone "No Ads" option. This removes all ads permanently on your account.

**I made a purchase but didn't receive my content.**
Please make sure you're connected to the internet when completing a purchase. If the issue persists, contact us below with your purchase receipt/date and we'll help right away.

**How do I change the app language or theme?**
Go to Settings from the main menu to change language, icon set, theme (White / Teal / Night), and sound/vibration preferences.
